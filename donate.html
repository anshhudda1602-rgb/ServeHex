Donation Guidelines

What you can donate, how to prepare items, and how pickup/drop works.

Accepted Items

  • Gently used clothes (washed, folded)
  • Blankets, sweaters, jackets
  • Shoes (paired, clean), bags
  • Household items in working condition

How It Works

  1. Search NGOs near you on the NGOs page
  2. Contact the NGO to confirm needs
  3. Arrange pickup or drop-off as per NGO policy
  4. Mark your donation as completed
